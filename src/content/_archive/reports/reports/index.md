CloudForecast delivers automated reports by email and Slack to help you and your team stay on top of cloud spending without spending more than a few minutes a day. Reports can be sent to different recipients and configured with thresholds that match your team's needs.

All CloudForecast plans include at least one overall cost report. Growth and Enterprise subscribers can create multiple reports segmented by tags and sub-accounts, each delivered to different team members.

---

## Report Types

### Daily Cost Report

A daily email (and optional Slack notification) that shows your cloud spend for the previous day, highlights any cost anomalies, and provides running monthly totals and forecasts. It is delivered each morning once AWS finalizes cost data for the prior day.

Recipients: anyone on your team who needs day-to-day cost visibility.

[Read the Daily Cost Report documentation](/features/reports/daily/)

---

### Weekly RI & SP Report

A weekly email delivered every Tuesday that proactively reviews your Reserved Instance and Savings Plan purchases. It alerts you to underutilized reservations, missed savings opportunities, and upcoming expirations.

Recipients: engineering leads, FinOps practitioners, or anyone managing reservation purchases.

Available on Growth and Enterprise plans.

[Read the Weekly RI & SP Report documentation](/features/reports/weekly-ri/)

---

### Monthly Financial Report (CFO Report)

A monthly Excel report delivered between the 6th and 13th of each month once your previous month's costs are finalized. It provides a high-level financial overview of your AWS costs across four sheets, designed for finance teams and executives.

Recipients: finance teams, CFOs, and other stakeholders who need a monthly cost summary.

[Read the Monthly Financial Report documentation](/features/reports/monthly/)

---

### Monthly Tagging Compliance Report

A monthly Excel report that measures how well your AWS resources conform to your tagging policy. It identifies untagged and incorrectly tagged resources so your team can address gaps.

Recipients: engineering leads and platform teams responsible for tagging governance.

Available on Enterprise plans only.

[Read the Monthly Tagging Compliance Report documentation](/features/reports/tagging-compliance/)
