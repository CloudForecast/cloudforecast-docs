description: "Understand the CloudForecast weekly Reserved Instance and Savings Plan report, including missed savings alerts, utilization metrics, and expiration tracking."
---

The Weekly RI & SP Report arrives every Tuesday and takes a proactive approach to your reservation investments. It surfaces underutilized reservations, missed savings opportunities, and upcoming expirations before they become problems.

> **Note:** This report is available on Growth and Enterprise plans only.

The report has two main sections:

1. Alerts
2. Reserved Instance Detail by Product

---

## Alerts

The alerts section highlights the areas where you have the most to gain. By default, CloudForecast alerts you when a Reserved Instance falls below its breakeven utilization point — the threshold at which the reservation is saving you money compared to on-demand pricing.

You can change the alert mode to a custom utilization percentage in your report configuration under **Reports → Weekly RI & SP Report**.

The top four missed savings opportunities are shown, each with the following details:

- Product
- Region
- Instance type
- RI Coverage
- RI Utilization
- RI Net Savings
- RI Missed Savings

Clicking **Detail** on any alert takes you directly to the corresponding section of the CloudForecast app for a deeper look.

---

## Reserved Instance Detail by Products

This section gives a full health summary of your Reserved Instances broken down by AWS product. CloudForecast reports on the following products:

- EC2
- Redshift
- RDS
- ElastiCache
- Elasticsearch

For each product, the report shows:

- RI Coverage
- RI Utilization
- RI Missed Savings
- On-Demand Cost
- Upcoming expirations (within 7, 30, and 60 days)
- Coverage and utilization broken down by region
