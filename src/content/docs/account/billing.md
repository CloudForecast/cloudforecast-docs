---

CloudForecast billing is managed under **Account → Billing**. From there you can view your current plan status, access invoices, and update your payment method.

---

## Free Trial

All new CloudForecast accounts start with a **30-day free trial**. No credit card is required to get started. You will have full access to the features included in your selected plan during the trial period.

---

## Viewing Your Plan and Invoices

Go to **Account → Billing** to see:

- Your current subscription plan and trial status.
- A table of all past invoices, including the date, amount, and payment status.
- A link to view or download each individual invoice.

---

## Adding or Updating a Payment Method

You can add or update your credit card at any time from the Billing page.

1. Go to **Account → Billing**.
2. Under **Payment Information**, enter your card number, expiry date, and CVC.
3. Click **Update**.

Your new card will be used for all future billing.

---

## What Happens When the Trial Ends

When your 30-day free trial ends, CloudForecast will prompt you to add a payment method to continue service. If your trial has already ended and you need to adjust your plan or discuss options, please contact us at [support@cloudforecast.io](mailto:support@cloudforecast.io).

---

## Billing Questions

For any billing questions, plan changes, or assistance, contact the CloudForecast team at [support@cloudforecast.io](mailto:support@cloudforecast.io).
