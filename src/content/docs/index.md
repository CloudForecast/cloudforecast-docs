## Features

Learn what CloudForecast can do for your team.

| Feature | Description | FinOps Framework |
|---|---|---|
| [Cost Groups](/features/cost-groups/) | Define scoped views of your cloud spend that power all reports, alerts, and dashboards. | Allocation |
| [Cost Detective](/features/cost-detective/) | Interactively drill into cost spikes and anomalies across all your connected data sources. | Anomaly Management |
| [Cost Increase Alerts](/features/cost-increase-alerts/) | Automatically detect and notify your team the moment cloud spend rises unexpectedly. | Anomaly Management |
| [Cost Increase Investigation](/features/cost-increase-investigation/) | Get an auto-generated breakdown of what drove a cost spike, without writing a single query. | Anomaly Management |
| [ZeroWaste](/features/zerowaste/) | Surface actionable rightsizing and waste-reduction recommendations across your AWS environment. | Usage Optimization |
| [RI & SP Inventory](/features/ri-sp-inventory/) | Track every Reserved Instance and Savings Plan in one place with expiration alerts and utilization health. | Rate Optimization |
| [RI & SP Usage Dashboard](/features/ri-sp-usage/) | Measure compute coverage, savings impact, and per-commitment utilization across your portfolio. | Rate Optimization |
| [MAP Tracker](/features/map-tracker/) | Monitor AWS Migration Acceleration Program credit consumption and spend targets throughout your migration. | Rate Optimization |
| [AWS Marketplace Dashboard](/features/marketplace/) | Track upfront fees, monthly usage, and daily trends for all Marketplace subscriptions in one view. | Reporting & Analytics |
| [Monthly Financial Report](/features/monthly-financial-report/) | Receive a structured Excel workbook each month with executive summaries, top movers, and service-level cost breakdowns. | Reporting & Analytics |
| [Tagging Center](/features/tagging-center/) | Define your tagging policy, measure compliance, and identify untagged resources dragging down cost attribution. | Allocation |
| [EKS & ECS Container Cost Visibility](/features/container-costs/) | Attribute shared container costs down to the pod, namespace, and service level using AWS Split Cost Allocation Data. | Allocation |
| [Databricks Cost Dashboard](/features/databricks/) | Understand DBU costs by workspace, cluster, and owner alongside underlying cloud infrastructure spend. | Reporting & Analytics |

## Integrations

Connect CloudForecast to the tools your team already uses.

- [Slack](/integrations/slack/) — Receive daily cost reports and spend alerts directly in your Slack channels.
- [Microsoft Teams](/integrations/teams/) — Deliver CloudForecast reports and alerts to a Teams channel via incoming webhook.
- [Jira](/integrations/jira/) — Push ZeroWaste optimization recommendations directly into Jira issues for engineering follow-through.
- [ProsperOps](/integrations/prosperops/) — See ProsperOps savings metrics alongside your AWS costs in Cost Group reports.
- [Cortex](/integrations/cortex/) — Surface CloudForecast Cost Group data inside Cortex so engineers see cloud costs next to their services.
